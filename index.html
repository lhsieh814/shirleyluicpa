Company History

Shirley graduated from Concordia University with a Bachelor of commerce in Account. Immediately after, got recognized as a General Certified Accountant (CGA). She started working in Philip Yip's accounting firm and slowly promoted to partner position. After realizing that Philip was not doing his share of the work, she decided to leave and start her own accounting firm. Along with her trusty side-kick, Alison Chik, and about a handfull of clients under her arms, she left to Vieux Port of Montreal.
